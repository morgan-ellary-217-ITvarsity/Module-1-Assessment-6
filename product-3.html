Description

It packs everything you'd expect from a 2020 flagship - Snapdragon 865 SoC, 108MP main camera aided by a macro and ultra-wide although, there's no proper telephoto. However, one would argue that cropping from the 108MP main sensor would probably net better results than a cheap 2x zoom camera. There's also a significant increase in battery capacity over the last generation jumping to 5,000 mAh and last, but not least, a high-refresh-rate display - a 144Hz one at that.
