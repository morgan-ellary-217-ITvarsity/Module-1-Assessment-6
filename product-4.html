Description

The OnePlus 8T was released in April, it has a similar design to the 8, but carries over some of the better specs of the 8 Pro. This premium hardware includes a silky 120Hz display and four cameras on the back. It also comes with a beefy 65-watt charger that juices up the phone quickly. The OnePlus 8T has a few software updates, too, including an always-on display and a refreshed interface, which will roll out to other OnePlus phones.
